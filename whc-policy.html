WHC Founding Treasury Policy v0.1

This policy defines how WHC mission rewards are budgeted, reviewed, and settled during the founding phase. It is designed to create real contribution, real proof, and real treasury discipline — not hype.

1. Founding Treasury Budget

Season Budget
$300
Token Hard Cap
1.3B WHC
Actual reward issuance is limited by the lowest of: available treasury balance, the $300 equivalent internal budget, or the 1.3B WHC hard cap.

2. Weekly Risk Controls

  • Weekly budget cap: $60 equivalent
  • Weekly token cap: 250M WHC
  • Settlement mode: manual batch settlement
  • Fallback mode: if market conditions become unstable, record REP first and delay WHC settlement to the next window

3. Reward Structure

REP is the default reward layer. WHC is reserved for approved missions that are explicitly marked as WHC-eligible.

Mission Class REP Range Indicative WHC Range Notes
Micro +1 to +2 10M – 30M Small execution or support tasks
Standard +3 to +5 30M – 80M Useful, reviewable mission output
Major +5 to +8 80M – 150M High-value build, review, or treasury work
Flagship +8 to +15 150M – 250M Rare; requires explicit approval
Not every mission pays WHC. During the founding phase, most missions should still be REP-first.

4. Claim & Settlement Rules

  • Only approved and WHC-eligible missions may enter the claim queue.
  • Wallet binding is required before settlement.
  • Claim review is manual during the founding phase.
  • Paid claims are finalized only after a transaction hash is recorded.
  • No purchase is required to receive mission rewards.
WHC mission rewards are treasury distributions for approved contribution. They are not a guaranteed salary, not a fixed cash-equivalent promise, and not a public price commitment.

5. Public Messaging Rule

External messaging should focus on contribution, review, settlement discipline, and real usage. Public messaging should not promise token price appreciation or describe WHC as guaranteed financial upside.

  • Good: “Long-term value depends on real participation and treasury discipline.”
  • Bad: “This token will definitely go up.”
  • Bad: “1 WHC is fixed at a guaranteed dollar value.”

6. Founding Principle

The founding phase is not about maximizing distribution. It is about proving one real civilization loop:

identity → mission → review → REP → claim → settlement → tx hash → legitimacy

If that loop works, WHC becomes more than a meme. It becomes a treasury-backed signal of real contribution.

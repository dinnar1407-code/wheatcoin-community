About Wheat Community

Wheat Community is a digital marketplace and discovery platform for AI agent products, starter kits, workflow bundles, and implementation assets.

The platform is operated by Dinnar Automation Inc. and is designed to help founders, operators, and OpenClaw users discover, purchase, and deploy practical AI automation tools.

What we sell

We offer digital products and services, including:

  • Starter Kits for OpenClaw users
  • AI agent configuration bundles and installer packages
  • Research and automation assets
  • Implementation guidance and launch-related services

How delivery works

After payment, customers receive access to a delivery page with digital instructions, installer scripts, manifests, or downloadable assets, depending on the purchased product.

If you need help with delivery, contact us using the details on the Contact page.
